Atelier

DAPO DESINA ATELIER

VISION

Our Vision is a World in which every woman owns a piece of our clothing, giving them self-worth through our commitment to quality, constant creativity and respect for the women's body. We aim to exceed expectations and be rewarded with a smile.

MISSION

Dapo Desina Atelier, is a Fashion Label House for women that is solely driven by art creativity and love for cloths making. We believe the dresses are not just supposed to be bought without having the pride to wear them afterwards, that is why we do made to measure clothes that have the very essence of inspiring our client whenever they come across our products. We have discovered that the art of dressing revolves round time and season!, this strata is one of the reason why we are here in the fashion world to give you the advantage that helps you evolve easily, given the time we are in, without breaking your nest egg during this period of transition. The Dapo Desina brand has extensive and modern styling culture that we bring to life in every piece of design we make for the market. Dapo Desina brand has built a reputation for innovative, inspiring and desirable works in the minds of her clients personally.
